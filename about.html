Menopause changed her hair. Nature changed the routine.

Our founder’s story

When shedding spiked during menopause, she went searching for a routine that felt simple and dependable. No complicated regimens—just a few natural oils with a long history of use.

What followed became Root Renewal: a small-batch blend of castor, batana, rosemary, and clove. It’s crafted to support a comfortable scalp and the look of stronger, fuller hair—a calm, consistent routine when everything else feels like it’s changing.

Small-batch care, made simple.

ET Naturals started with a straightforward goal: make a reliable, easy hair & scalp treatment with ingredients we can stand behind. Root Renewal is blended in small batches with castor, batana, rosemary, and clove — nothing complicated, just what works.

How we make it

  1. Source & verify. Oils are checked for identity and clarity before blending.
  2. Measure & blend. Controlled ratios by weight, mixed low-and-slow to preserve character.
  3. Rest & bottle. Short conditioning period before bottling into cobalt glass.
