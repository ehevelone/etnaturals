Our Story

We started ET Naturals because everyday products shouldn’t force you to choose between effectiveness and peace of mind. Living with chronic pain and neurodiversity in our home made us rethink ingredients and simplify routines—so we built formulas that are gentle, straightforward, and actually work.

Why we started

We were tired of cabinets full of complicated, chemical-heavy products. Our founder lives with fibromyalgia, and in our family we also navigate ADD/ADHD—so “simple and gentle” isn’t a nice-to-have, it’s essential. A well-loved book of natural remedies inspired us to craft small-batch formulas at home, then share what worked with others.

What we make

Everyday essentials made from a short list of plant-forward ingredients. We started with Root Renewal, our lightweight scalp serum designed to support a healthier-looking scalp and reduce breakage. We’re carefully expanding into companion hair-care products that fit the same philosophy: simple, gentle, effective.

Our standards

  • Short, readable ingredient lists
  • No parabens, phthalates, or artificial dyes
  • Small-batch, consistent quality
  • Clear labeling and plain-English directions
  • Cosmetic use only — always patch test first
