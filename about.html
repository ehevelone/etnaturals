Our Story

Less harsh stuff. More things that actually work.

ET Naturals started at our kitchen table with one goal: make everyday products gentler—without sacrificing results.

Shop products Read testimonials

Why we started

Like many families, we got tired of feeling “forced” to choose products loaded with harsh chemicals, dyes, and fillers. Living with fibromyalgia—and raising kids while navigating ADD/ADHD—made us extra mindful of what comes into our home. That search for simpler options led our founder to a well-worn book of natural remedies and a lot of weekend experiments.

We began making household staples and personal-care basics that kept our routines simple and our ingredient lists short. Friends started asking for refills, and before long ET Naturals was born so we could share those same everyday helpers with you.

What we make

  • Plant-forward hair and scalp care (like Root Renewal)
  • Low-fragrance, dye-free essentials made in small batches
  • Practical, easy-to-use formulas designed for real life

We’re starting with personal care and expanding thoughtfully.

Our standards

  • No parabens, phthalates, or artificial dyes
  • Short, readable ingredient lists—nothing to hide
  • Tested in our own home before it ever makes it to yours
  • Clear directions and realistic expectations

A note on wellness

We believe gentle, consistent routines can make a big difference. Our products are cosmetic and household in nature— they aren’t intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. If you have specific health questions, please consult your healthcare provider.
