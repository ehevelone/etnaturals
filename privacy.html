Privacy Policy

Last updated:

We respect your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Who we are

ET Naturals (“we”, “us”, “our”). Contact: etnaturals@outlook.com.

Information we collect

  • Orders/Payments. When you purchase, you’re redirected to PayPal or Venmo. Those providers process your payment details. We do not receive or store card numbers or bank info.
  • Testimonials. If you submit a testimonial (Google Forms), we collect what you enter (e.g., name, location, rating, comments). Submissions are reviewed before publishing.
  • Messages & email. If you email us or submit a site form, we keep the info needed to reply and support you.
  • Basic usage data. Our host (Netlify) may log standard data (IP, browser, pages viewed) for security/operations.

How we use information

  • Fulfill and support orders.
  • Moderate and display testimonials you approve for publication.
  • Respond to questions and provide customer service.
  • Improve our website and prevent abuse/fraud.

Sharing

We share data only with service providers we use to run the site:

  • PayPal / Venmo for payments.
  • Google Forms & Sheets for testimonial collection/moderation.
  • Netlify for site hosting (and Netlify Forms, if enabled).

We don’t sell your personal information.

Cookies

Our pages are mostly cookie-light. Third-party services (e.g., PayPal, Venmo, Google) may set cookies when their content is used. You can control cookies in your browser settings.

Data retention

Order/support records are kept as needed for business, legal, and tax purposes. Testimonials are kept until you ask us to remove them or we remove them during moderation.

Your choices

  • Request a copy, correction, or deletion of your info: etnaturals@outlook.com.
  • Ask us to remove a published testimonial anytime.

If you’re in a region with specific privacy rights (e.g., California/EEA), contact us and we’ll honor applicable requests.

Children

Our products and site aren’t directed to children under 13. We don’t knowingly collect children’s personal information.

Changes

We may update this policy as we add features or services. We’ll post updates here and adjust the “Last updated” date.
