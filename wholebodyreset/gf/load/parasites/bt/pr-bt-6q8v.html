When Things Feel Significantly Better

Feeling better does not mean the work is finished. It means the body is cooperating.

At this stage, many people feel tempted to: increase herbs, skip binders, or “push the cleanse.”

This is where most relapses begin.

Parasites adapt quickly. Rapid improvement can create a false sense of completion.

Holding steady allows deeper layers to surface safely.

Continue the same cycle. Maintain hydration and binders. Let the body complete what it started.

Advancement happens later — not here.
