No Major Changes Yet — That’s Okay

This page is part of Guided Foundations. There is no expectation to feel dramatic change at this stage.

Many people do not feel immediate shifts when beginning parasite work — especially when the body is prioritizing stabilization over elimination.

Some people notice subtle signs instead:

  • slightly steadier energy
  • less reactivity to foods or stress
  • more predictable digestion
  • better tolerance of binders

These signals don’t mean parasites aren’t present — they often mean the body is choosing regulation first.

No change does not mean failure. It often means the terrain is still being prepared.
