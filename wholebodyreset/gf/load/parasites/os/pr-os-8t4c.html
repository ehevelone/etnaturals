When Detox Feels Like Too Much

Parasite die-off can release ammonia, metals, and inflammatory compounds. If binders or hydration aren’t keeping pace, symptoms increase.

Helpful adjustments often include:

  • reducing herb frequency
  • increasing hydration
  • adding or spacing binders
  • taking a full rest day

This is how the body regains balance without shutting detox down entirely.

Slower often leads to better long-term progress.
