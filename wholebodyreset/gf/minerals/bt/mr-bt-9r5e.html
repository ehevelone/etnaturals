Stability Is a Win

Not all improvement feels exciting.

With minerals, progress often shows up as fewer swings rather than a sudden boost.

Stabilizing Signs May Include

  • More predictable sleep
  • Less anxiety or nervousness
  • Reduced cravings
  • Fewer “crashes” during the day

These shifts suggest balance is improving.

When stability increases, the body is building capacity.

Capacity matters more than intensity.
