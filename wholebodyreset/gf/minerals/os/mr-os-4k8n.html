Your Body Is Adjusting

Mineral shifts can feel uncomfortable before they feel supportive.

Some people notice that adding or changing minerals creates sensations they weren’t expecting.

Common Early Signals

  • Heart palpitations or fluttering
  • Feeling wired, anxious, or jittery
  • Muscle tension or twitching
  • Digestive changes

These are not emergencies. They are signs that balance is still shifting.

The goal here is not to push through discomfort, but to allow the body time to recalibrate.

Slower changes are often better tolerated.

Educational content only. Not medical advice. No immediate action or adjustment is required.
