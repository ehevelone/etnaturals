When Balance Tips Too Far

Minerals work in relationship with each other. Too much of one can feel just as uncomfortable as not enough.

Some People Notice

  • Loose stools (often magnesium-related)
  • Fatigue or heaviness
  • Brain fog or sluggishness
  • Feeling “off” without a clear reason

These signals are information — not failure.

Often, the most supportive response is simply reducing intensity and allowing balance to return.

Minerals respond best to patience.
