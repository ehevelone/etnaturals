Parasite Die-Off vs Overload

This page contains educational material from the Whole Body Reset – Foundations text. There is no requirement to implement anything here. Read and apply only what feels supportive.

Why This Page Exists

Many people stop parasite support too early — or push far too hard — because they cannot tell the difference between healthy die-off and nervous system overload.

This page exists to slow that process down and protect your body.

What Healthy Die-Off Often Feels Like

  • Temporary fatigue that improves with rest
  • Mild headaches that resolve with hydration
  • Increased bowel movements or detox output
  • Short-lived flu-like sensations

These symptoms are usually time-limited and improve with binders, hydration, and rest.

What Overload or Overstimulation Feels Like

  • Escalating anxiety or panic
  • Insomnia or racing thoughts
  • Heart palpitations or shakiness
  • Persistent nausea or dizziness
  • Symptoms that worsen instead of stabilizing

These are not signs to “push through.” They are signs to slow down.

What to Do If Things Feel Too Intense

  • Pause parasite-focused herbs
  • Increase binder support
  • Return focus to hydration and minerals
  • Support the nervous system and kidneys

Backing up does not erase progress. It preserves it.

Important Reminder

The goal of parasite work is not suffering. It is clearance without panic.
