Parasite Symptoms & Patterns

This page contains educational material from the Whole Body Reset – Foundations text. There is no requirement to implement anything here. Read and apply only what feels supportive.

Parasites often operate quietly and cyclically. Symptoms may come and go, intensify around stress, or flare in predictable patterns.

Common Parasite Signs

  • Bloating, especially after meals
  • Constipation or diarrhea
  • Foul-smelling gas
  • Sugar cravings
  • Waking between 2–3 a.m.
  • Teeth grinding
  • Itchy skin, scalp, or ears
  • Rashes that come and go
  • “Crawling” sensations
  • Anxiety spikes
  • Muscle twitches
  • Migraines
  • Brittle nails
  • Nutrient deficiencies despite a healthy diet
  • Unexplained weight changes

Full Moon Flare Patterns

  • Insomnia or vivid dreams
  • Nightmares
  • Digestive discomfort
  • Mood swings
  • Heightened anxiety

These patterns do not confirm infestation, but they are common indicators that parasite support may need to be considered carefully.
