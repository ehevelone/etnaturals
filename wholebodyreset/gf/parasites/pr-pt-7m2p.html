Parasite Protocol & Binder Support

This page contains educational material from the Whole Body Reset – Foundations text. There is no requirement to implement anything here. Read and apply only what feels supportive.

Parasite support must always include binding. Killing or mobilizing organisms without binders increases toxin recirculation and symptom severity.

Core Rule

Parasite work is never done without binders in place.

Foundational Parasite Support

  • Herbal parasite blend (low dose)
  • Evening dosing is often better tolerated
  • Support bowel movements daily

Binder Options

  • Activated charcoal
  • Bentonite clay
  • Zeolite
  • Chlorella (if tolerated)

Binders are taken away from food and supplements to avoid nutrient depletion.

Timing Considerations

  • Start low
  • Increase slowly
  • Cycle off regularly
  • Pause during intense reactions

Parasite work is rarely linear. The goal is reduction, not eradication.
