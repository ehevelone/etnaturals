Cilantro Detox Smoothie

A gentle, food-based metal support recipe used during metal detox phases and long-term maintenance.

Ingredients

  • 1 packed cup fresh cilantro
  • 1 banana
  • ½ cup pineapple (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 cup filtered water or coconut water
  • Optional: 1 tsp spirulina (additional binding support)

Instructions

Blend all ingredients until smooth. Drink immediately for best effectiveness.

Why this works:

  • Cilantro gently pulls heavy metals from tissues
  • Pineapple supports digestion and lymph flow
  • Banana provides potassium for nervous system balance
  • Spirulina adds mild binding support (optional)

When to Use

  • During metal detox phases
  • During maintenance (2–3× per week)
  • After parasite cycles
  • After sweating or sauna

This smoothie is intentionally gentle. It supports detox without triggering aggressive mobilization.
