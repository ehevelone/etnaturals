Bentonite Clay Water

This binder recipe is used throughout Guided Foundations to prevent toxin, metal, and parasite waste recirculation.

Why Bentonite Clay Works

Bentonite clay carries a strong negative charge. This allows it to bind positively charged toxins such as:

  • heavy metals
  • parasite waste
  • ammonia and endotoxins
  • chemical residues

Clay does not kill anything. It acts as a safety net so toxins exit instead of recirculating.

How to Prepare Clay Water

  • 8 oz filtered water
  • ½–1 tsp food-grade bentonite clay

Add clay to a glass jar or ceramic cup. Stir with a wooden or plastic utensil. Never use metal.

How to Use

  • Take on an empty stomach
  • At least 1–2 hours away from food, supplements, or herbs
  • Follow with hydration water

How Often

  • Beginner: 2–3× per week
  • Active detox: daily as tolerated
  • Maintenance: 1–2× per week

If constipation occurs, increase hydration and reduce frequency.
