Cat’s Claw Immune Tea

A gentle but effective herbal support for EBV reactivation and immune regulation.

Why This Works

Cat’s Claw is a well-known immune modulator. It helps reduce viral activity without overstimulating the immune system — making it especially useful during EBV-related fatigue, inflammation, or flares.

  • Supports immune balance
  • Helps calm EBV reactivation
  • Reduces inflammatory load
  • Pairs well with binders and hydration

Recipe

  • 1 tsp cat’s claw bark or powder
  • 1 tsp calendula (optional, for lymph support)
  • ½ tsp ginger (optional)
  • 8–12 oz filtered water

Preparation

Simmer herbs gently for 5–10 minutes. Strain and sip warm.

When to Use

  • During EBV support phases
  • When fatigue or gland swelling appears
  • During parasite or metal work if EBV symptoms flare

If anxiety, racing heart, or fatigue spikes: pause herbs and increase hydration + binders.
