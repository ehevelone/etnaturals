This page contains educational material from the Whole Body Reset – Foundations text. There is no requirement to implement anything here. Read and apply only what feels supportive.

Binders are substances traditionally used in terrain-based work to help carry unwanted material out of the body through normal elimination. Quality, purity, and sourcing matter more than quantity.

What to Look For

  • Third-party testing or transparent sourcing
  • Minimal fillers or additives
  • Clear labeling of origin and processing

Examples of Reputable Sources
