Parasites — A Terrain Perspective

Parasites are not rare, exotic, or shameful. They are opportunistic organisms that thrive in weakened terrain.

Why Parasites Matter

Parasites store heavy metals, release inflammatory waste, and interfere with digestion, hormones, and immunity.

Ignoring them often makes metal and viral work unstable.

Why Preparation Comes First
