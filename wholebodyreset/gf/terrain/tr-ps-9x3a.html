Pause & Support

This page appears when terrain work feels like too much. This is feedback, not failure.

Some people notice signs such as:

  • Increased fatigue
  • Anxiety or restlessness
  • Constipation
  • Swollen lymph nodes
  • Worsening sleep

These signals often indicate that the body needs more foundational support, not stronger intervention.

What This Means

The body may benefit from:

  • Slower pacing
  • More time with hydration and minerals
  • Reduced stimulation
  • Individualized guidance

Pausing protects the nervous system and prevents long-term setbacks.

If symptoms persist or feel concerning, consider working with a qualified natural practitioner or healthcare provider. Support is a strength, not a failure.
